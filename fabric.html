"Beauport Old Colony"
An anonymous Regency period design reissued in the early 20th century and used for curtains, bedhangings and bedspreads in a major bedchamber at Beauport, a property of the Society for the Preservation of New England Antiquities, in Gloucester, Massachusetts. 56 3/4" wide with a 1 3/4" repeat. On cotton sheeting, suitable for drapery. Hand printed in New England. Offered in brown on light beige (a document colorway) and indigo on light beige.
