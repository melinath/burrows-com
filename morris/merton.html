Merton Runners

This adaptation of a famous "Hammersmith" carpet design by William Morris is woven in Europe on a spool Axminster loom in a number of sizes of seamless rugs with authentic Morris coloring.

Available in four widths and in three colorways (red center, green center or blue center):

24" wide = $ 87.48 per yard
27" wide = $ 100.62 per yard
36" wide = $ 132.72 per yard
44" wide also available
