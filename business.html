Doing Business With Us

J.R. Burrows & Company markets by mail order to the retail public as well as to the design trade. Our office is usually open from 9:00 am to 5:30 pm, Monday - Friday. If we are not present to take a telephone call, please leave a message or contact us by e-mail. We accept VISA, Mastercard and American Express, but advise for security reasons that you do not send your credit card number via e-mail.

We have a small showroom at our office in Rockland, and welcome visitors - but we highly recommend making a call first to set an appointment and receive directions.
