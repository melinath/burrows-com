Burrows Studios: Ginko Leaf
Color: Celadon

Trimmed Width: 27"

Repeat: 8 1/4" self match

5 foot rolls

Price per roll: $135.00

"Ginko Leaf", a Japanese design. Published in 1885 in the New York magazine Art & Decoration, this Japanese design was suggested for manufacture as a wallpaper by the editor, George Halm. 27" wide with a self match repeat of 8 1/4". 5 yard long rolls, covering 30 square feet. Hand printed in New England. Offered in shades of pale gold on a cream ground, and in celadon green. $135.00 per roll.
Celadon
