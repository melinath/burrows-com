Burrows Studio: Peruvian Lily
from the Norwood-Day Collection
Peruvian Lily

Trimmed width: 21"

Repeat: 20 7/8" self match

Roll size: 6 yards

Price per roll: $155.00

"Peruvian Lily," an English design. The flower of this design from c. 1890 was identified by friend who is a horticulturist in Oakland, California. Abstracted in the manner of the finest Arts & Crafts Movement designs, this pattern has a simplicity and elegance that is achieve with the simple use of color. 21" wide with a self match repeat of 20 7/8". 6 yard long rolls, covering 30 square feet. Hand printed in New England. Offered with off white figure on a pale grey-blue ground (the document coloring) and an off white figure on a moss green ground. A third colorway has a warm golden yellow ground with white figures. $155.00 per roll.
