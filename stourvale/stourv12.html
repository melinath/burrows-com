Codman Turkey (Kykuit)

Designed c. 1910, installed 1913 at Kykuit, the John D. Rockefeller House, Pocantico Hills Estate, New York.

Body: 91325/10; 27" wide (3/4 width)

Repeat: 48" drop match

Border: 91325/18; 18" wide, woven 1/over (3/4 width)

McBride Geometric

Designed c. 1910

Body: 9954/10

Border: none

TITANIC: It has long been said that carpet for the Titanic was woven at Stourvale Mill, though there is no definate proof. However, a famous photograph of a 2nd class cabin of the sister ship OLYMPIC clearly shows this design. A period color drawing of a 2nd class cabin shows the original colorway to be in red. Research in the design archive is underway to locate more Titanic designs.
